About

AHPMUN (American Heritage Plantation Model United Nations) is a high school Model UN conference held at American Heritage High School (Plantation) from September 29-30, 2018. For the second time, AHPMUN is open to non-Heritage students, presenting delegates with an opportunity to engage in lively, competitive debate. The Secretariat is composed of students from American Heritage's nationally ranked Model United Nations Team.
